Do's And Dont's

In Creating Your First

Youtube Video



Being a beginner on youtube can be pretty daunting, but
we've listed out a couple of Do's and Dont's to get you
started!


Do's



  • Identify Purpose and Audience

    • This is a no-brainer. You're probably reading this already with a theme in mind.
      Whether it be gaming, vlogging, or any other type of content, it's important to know
      which subject you'll be diving in, in order to reel in your viewers.

  • Creating An Outline

    • Creating a plan for your videos or content is another important step in order to create the high-quality videos that you
      see out there. Make sure to have all your ideas laid out to make the process easier overall.

  • Editing

    • Have you ever watched a movie, or a really good youtube video, and wondered: "what makes this so good?"
      Well, that's what editing is for. Editing is the process of making your video more appealing to your audience."

  • Optimizing Videos for SEO

    • Search Engine Optimization is essentially a way of improving your content's visibility
      on search engine results pages. A way of doing this is adding tags and description that
      accurately describe your content.

  • Use Catchy Thumbnails and Titles

    • Have a quick scroll through your Youtube feed. What's the first thing that caches your eye? Right!
      Thumbnails are a huge part of attracting viewers.are the looks that attract the viewers,
      think of title's as the first words you say to someone when making an impression.

  • Engage With Audience

    • Engaging is an important step because it keeps the viewers entertained and feel seen.
      Not just that, this type of interaction adheres to the Youtube algorithm, making your videos pop up
      in a lot more poeple's feeds.


Dont's


  • Don't One Take It

    • Doin your whole video in one take ensures a couple problems.
      It could cause confusion during editing, and can overall be less appealing to watch.

  • Don't Expect Virality

    • All of us dream of becoming that overnight sensation, but remember,
      it's almost impossible to gain overnight virality, So chin up. Put in the hard work, and
      you'll surely get your spot.

  • Don't Ignore Feedback

    • Remember when we talked about engagement? well those same comments contain feedback
      that could help you iprove. So remember to pay extra attention to those, as you could benefit
      from them greatly.

  • Don't Be Scared To Take A Leap

    • I hope we have'nt discouraged you after all those.
      Remember, don't be scared to take a leap, and do what you feel is right, and you think is best for your content.
      Have fun!
